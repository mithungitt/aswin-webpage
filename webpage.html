Hi, I'm Aswin Krishna!

In my research, I study the strain dynamics of Streptococcus pneumoniae and the mechanisms that maintain very high levels of antigenic diversity in this species. My perspective focuses on the role of within-host strain competition on the maintenance of this diversity.


I am also interested in antibiotic resistance and explaining patterns of resistance observed on data.
